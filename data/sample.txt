Remote Work, Vacation, and Expense Policy Document

Remote Work Policy
AcmeTech values work-life balance and supports remote work to foster productivity and well-being. Eligible employees (full-time and part-time, excluding positions requiring physical presence for operational reasons) may work remotely up to three days per week, pending manager approval. Employees are expected to maintain core working hours (9:00 am – 5:00 pm, local time), ensure a reliable internet connection, and make themselves available for virtual meetings and collaboration during business hours. Permanent remote work arrangements require submission of a formal request to the Human Resources (HR) department, reviewed in consultation with department managers. All remote work must comply with company security, confidentiality, and data privacy policies. Employees must use company-provided devices and are prohibited from accessing sensitive data on public Wi-Fi without an approved VPN.

Vacation Days Policy
To encourage rest and work-life balance, AcmeTech grants all regular employees 15 paid vacation days per calendar year. Vacation days are pro-rated for employees starting mid-year. In addition to paid vacation, employees will receive off on all recognized federal holidays. Vacation requests must be submitted at least two weeks in advance through the HR portal and approved by direct supervisors to ensure adequate team coverage. Unused vacation days can be carried over to the next calendar year up to a maximum of five days; any additional unused days will be forfeited. Emergency leave for special circumstances may be granted at management discretion.

Expense Reimbursement Policy
AcmeTech reimburses employees for reasonable, pre-approved business expenses directly related to their job duties. The maximum reimbursable amount is $500 per calendar month, inclusive of meals, transportation, lodging, and minor office supplies. Expenses must be approved by a supervisor in advance, except in urgent cases, and reimbursement claims must be submitted within 30 days using the company’s online expense portal, accompanied by itemized receipts. Expenses over $100 require explicit email authorization from a manager. Unauthorized or late submissions may not be reimbursed. All expenses are subject to audit at the company’s discretion.

Equipment Allowance Policy
New employees are eligible for a one-time equipment allowance of up to $1,000. This allowance covers the purchase of essential office equipment (laptops, monitors, ergonomic chairs, desks) necessary for productive remote work. Equipment purchases must be approved by the IT department to ensure compatibility and security standards. Employees seeking to use their allowance must submit a request with itemized quotes for approval prior to purchase. All equipment, unless otherwise agreed in writing, remains the property of AcmeTech and must be returned upon separation from the company or at the company’s request.

General Provisions
Policy violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination. Employees should contact HR with any questions or clarifications regarding remote work, vacation, expense, or equipment policies.
